Our solution

Built for Zambia’s urban water reality

Maji-Flow addresses aged infrastructure, high non-revenue water, payment friction, and limited field visibility with a practical digital layer that utilities and communities can use immediately.

💧

Smart Metering

IoT-enabled prepaid meters provide real-time flow tracking, usage visibility, and remote status checks for kiosks and shared access points.

📱

Instant Top-ups

Residents can purchase water instantly through Airtel Money, MTN MoMo, and Zamtel integrations—reducing queues and improving payment consistency.

Leak Detection

AI-powered flow analysis flags unusual patterns early, helping utilities respond faster to hidden leaks, theft, and pressure-related losses.
